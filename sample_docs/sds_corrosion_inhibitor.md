# Safety Data Sheet - Corrosion Inhibitor CI-5000

## Section 1: Identification

- **Product Name**: CI-5000 Corrosion Inhibitor
- **Product Code**: CI5000-25L
- **Manufacturer**: ChemGuard Industries
- **Emergency Phone**: +1-800-424-9300

## Section 2: Hazard Identification

**Classification**: Skin Irritant (Category 2), Eye Irritant (Category 2)

**Signal Word**: WARNING

**Hazard Statements**:

- H315: Causes skin irritation
- H319: Causes serious eye irritation
- H412: Harmful to aquatic life with long-lasting effects

**Precautionary Statements**:

- P264: Wash hands thoroughly after handling
- P280: Wear protective gloves/eye protection
- P305+P351+P338: IF IN EYES: Rinse cautiously with water for several minutes
- P332+P313: If skin irritation occurs: Get medical attention

## Section 4: First Aid Measures

### Inhalation

- Move to fresh air
- If breathing is difficult, give oxygen
- Seek medical attention if symptoms persist

### Skin Contact

- Remove contaminated clothing
- Wash with soap and plenty of water for 15 minutes
- Seek medical attention if irritation develops

### Eye Contact

- Rinse immediately with water for at least 15 minutes
- Hold eyelids open to ensure thorough rinsing
- Seek immediate medical attention

### Ingestion

- Do NOT induce vomiting
- Rinse mouth with water
- Seek immediate medical attention
- Never give anything by mouth to an unconscious person

## Section 5: Fire-Fighting Measures

- **Suitable Extinguishing Media**: Foam, CO2, dry chemical, water spray
- **Unsuitable Media**: Do not use water jet
- **Flash Point**: 62°C (144°F)
- **Auto-ignition Temperature**: Not determined
- **Flammability Limits**: LEL: 1.2%, UEL: 7.0%

**Special Hazards**:

- Combustion may produce toxic fumes of nitrogen oxides
- Container may rupture under fire conditions

**Firefighter Protection**:

- Wear self-contained breathing apparatus (SCBA)
- Full protective clothing required

## Section 6: Accidental Release Measures

### Personal Precautions

- Evacuate personnel to safe area
- Ensure adequate ventilation
- Wear appropriate PPE (gloves, goggles, respirator if needed)
- Eliminate all ignition sources

### Environmental Precautions

- Prevent entry into waterways, sewers, basements
- Notify authorities if product enters environment

### Cleanup Methods

- Contain spill with absorbent material (vermiculite, sand)
- Collect in suitable containers for disposal
- Wash spill area with water after pickup
- Dispose of per local regulations

## Section 7: Handling and Storage

### Handling

- Use in well-ventilated area
- Avoid contact with skin and eyes
- Do not eat, drink, or smoke when using
- Wash hands thoroughly after handling
- Use proper grounding/bonding techniques

### Storage

- Store in original container, tightly closed
- Keep in cool, dry, well-ventilated area
- Store away from incompatible materials
- Storage temperature: 5-40°C
- Keep containers upright to prevent leakage

## Section 9: Physical and Chemical Properties

- **Appearance**: Clear amber liquid
- **Odor**: Amine-like
- **pH**: 9.5-10.5 (1% solution)
- **Boiling Point**: >100°C
- **Specific Gravity**: 0.98 at 20°C
- **Vapor Pressure**: <10 mmHg at 20°C
- **Solubility**: Fully miscible in water
- **Viscosity**: 15-25 cP at 20°C

## Section 10: Stability and Reactivity

- **Stability**: Stable under normal conditions
- **Conditions to Avoid**: Heat, sparks, open flame
- **Incompatible Materials**:
  - Strong oxidizers
  - Strong acids
  - Halogens
- **Hazardous Decomposition**:
  - Carbon oxides (CO, CO2)
  - Nitrogen oxides (NOx)
- **Hazardous Polymerization**: Will not occur

## Section 11: Toxicological Information

- **Acute Oral LD50 (rat)**: >5000 mg/kg (low toxicity)
- **Acute Dermal LD50 (rabbit)**: >2000 mg/kg
- **Skin Irritation (rabbit)**: Mild irritant
- **Eye Irritation (rabbit)**: Moderate irritant
- **Sensitization**: Not a skin sensitizer

**Chronic Effects**: None known from long-term exposure

## Section 12: Ecological Information

- **Aquatic Toxicity (96h LC50 fish)**: 50-100 mg/L (harmful)
- **Biodegradability**: Readily biodegradable (>60% in 28 days)
- **Bioaccumulation**: Low potential (log Kow <3)
- **Mobility**: High mobility in soil
